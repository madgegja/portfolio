cs-ai-chatbot

AI-powered CS chatbot with Claude API + RAG. Handles 10 markets, 10 languages, fraud detection, and smart escalation. Targets 38% self-service automation from 11,621 real ticket analysis.

AI Python FastAPI
PROBLEM

1,600+ monthly tickets across 10 markets in 10 languages. Agents handling repetitive queries manually with inconsistent quality and slow response.

STRATEGY

Designed hybrid AI chatbot — 90% template + 10% Claude AI glue. 3-tier routing (auto/draft/escalate) with real-time fraud scoring and RAG knowledge base.

38% self-service target (~630 tickets/month). 161 test cases, full CI/CD with Docker. 9 Red Flag fraud detection system operational.
